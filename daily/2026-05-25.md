### 答题框架（Part 2 — Describe a piece of technology you use often）

**关键词方向：**
- What & Since when: 这个技术是什么 + 你从什么时候开始用（选你真实在用的——AI Agent / Vibe Coding 工具 / Hermes）
- How you use it: 每天怎么用，解决什么具体问题。给 2 个真实场景，别编
- Why you like it: 为什么觉得好用——选 1 个功能性原因 + 1 个情感原因
- Impact: 它改变了你工作/学习的什么方面？（展示思考深度）

**可用句型：**
- I started using... about... ago, and honestly, I can't imagine going back.
- It's not that it does anything magical — it's more that it removes the friction of...
- What surprised me most was how quickly it became part of my daily workflow.
- Looking back, I'd say the biggest change has been...

**Part 3 追问预判：**
- How has AI changed the way people work? → automation of routine tasks / new skill demands / remote collaboration
- Do you think AI will replace human jobs? → **你的主场！** balanced view: some jobs transformed, not eliminated; creativity and judgment still human domain
- What are the risks of depending too much on technology? → privacy / digital divide / skill atrophy / over-reliance
- Should children be taught to code from a young age? → computational thinking yes, but not at expense of creativity and social skills
- How do you think technology will change education in the future? → personalized learning / AI tutors / but human connection irreplaceable

**⚡ 冲刺提醒：** 这个话题是你最强的——你在学 AI，每天用 AI 工具。不要背答案，用你自己的真实经历。真实 > 华丽。录音后标记 3 个"卡顿了但本可以更顺"的地方，明天改。

## ✍️ 写作
- 类型：**Task 1 — 图表作文**
- 题目：

> **The chart below shows the percentage of households with internet access in three countries between 2005 and 2023.**
>
> Summarize the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.
>
> | Year | Country A | Country B | Country C |
> |------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
> | 2005 | 25% | 55% | 10% |
> | 2008 | 40% | 65% | 20% |
> | 2011 | 55% | 72% | 30% |
> | 2014 | 68% | 78% | 45% |
> | 2017 | 78% | 82% | 55% |
> | 2020 | 85% | 88% | 68% |
> | 2023 | 92% | 90% | 78% |

- 用时：
- 词数：
- 自评：

### Outline

| 段落 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| **Intro** | Paraphrase: The line graph illustrates the proportion of households connected to the internet across three countries over an 18-year period from 2005 to 2023. |
| **Overview** | — All three countries saw significant growth. Country B had the highest starting point and remained top but was nearly caught by A. Country C had the lowest starting point but grew fastest. — The gap between the three narrowed considerably over time. |
| **Body 1** | Detail Country A & B: B started at 55% and rose steadily to 90% (but growth slowed after 2014 → only +12pp in final 9 years). A started at 25% and surged to 92% — overtaking B in 2023. A's growth was more linear and aggressive. |
| **Body 2** | Detail Country C + comparison: C started at just 10% — the digital divide was stark. But C grew the fastest: from 10% to 78% (nearly 8x). Most rapid growth was 2011-2017 (+25pp). By 2023, the gap between highest (A, 92%) and lowest (C, 78%) was only 14pp — compared to 45pp in 2005. |

**高分词汇备选：**
- rose steadily / experienced a dramatic surge / saw the most rapid growth
- the gap narrowed considerably / the digital divide
- overtook / surpassed / was overtaken by
- accounted for / represented / stood at
- the figure climbed from A to B / increased nearly eightfold

**⚡ 冲刺提醒：**
- 限时 **18 分钟**（考试 20，冲刺加压）
- Task 1 **Overview 是得分关键**——没有 overview = 最高 5 分。Overview 在第一段或第二段都行，但必须出现
- 不要罗列每一个数据点——选趋势和对比。考官要看"你读懂了图表"，不是"你会报数字"
- 数据引用格式：25% → "a quarter" / 50% → "half" / 75% → "three quarters" / "approximately two thirds" / "just under one in ten"
- 150 词以上即可，不超 200。简洁 > 冗长
