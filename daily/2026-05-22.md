### 答题框架（Part 2 — Describe an environmental problem）

**关键词方向：**
- What: 环境问题名称 + 位置/范围（air pollution / plastic waste / deforestation / climate change）
- Cause: 主要原因（industrial / consumer behavior / policy gap）
- Effect: 对人们/生态的影响（health / economy / wildlife）
- Solution: 目前措施 or 你认为该怎么做

**可用句型：**
- One environmental issue that concerns me is...
- The main cause is... / This is largely due to...
- As a result, ... has been affected / we've seen...
- To tackle this, ... / What we really need is...

**Part 3 追问预判：**
- What should individuals do to protect the environment?
- Should governments punish companies that pollute? → agree/disagree + example
- Is economic growth always at odds with environmental protection? → nuance 题，准备"短期冲突/长期统一"的观点
- Do you think AI can help solve environmental problems? → **结合你的 AI 学习背景，天然素材！**

## 写作
- 类型：**Task 2 — Opinion Essay**
- 题目：

> **Some people think that environmental problems are too big for individuals to solve, and that only governments and large companies can make a difference. To what extent do you agree or disagree?**

- 用时：
- 词数：
- 自评：

### Outline

| 段落 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| Intro | Paraphrase + state position: disagree — individuals matter too, but all three levels need to work together |
| Body 1 | Why people think only governments/companies matter — scale, regulation, funding |
| Body 2 | What individuals CAN do — consumer choices, lifestyle changes, voting, community action, social pressure on companies |
| Body 3 | The multiplier effect — individual action × millions = systemic change; individuals drive demand that shapes corporate behavior |
| Conclusion | Individual action is necessary but not sufficient — all three must collaborate |

**高分词汇备选：**
- collective impact / grassroots movements
- consumer-driven change
- policy enforcement / regulatory framework
- carbon footprint / sustainable alternatives
- ripple effect / tipping point

**⚡ 强化提醒：** 限时 25 分钟。别写太抽象——每个论点配一个具体例子。Intro 2 句就够了，别啰嗦。
