### 答题框架（Part 2 — Describe your job or studies）

**关键词方向：**
- What: 职业/专业名称 + 一句话定位
- Why: 为什么选这个（兴趣/机会/影响力）
- How: 日常做什么 + 喜欢/不喜欢的点
- Future: 未来打算（短期/长期）

**可用句型：**
- I'm currently... / I work as...
- What I find most rewarding is...
- The main challenge I face is...
- In the future, I hope to...

**Part 3 追问预判：**
- How has technology changed your field?
- Do you think AI will replace jobs like yours? → 结合 Web3/AI 背景，有天然优势

## 写作
- 类型：Task 2 — 议论文

> **Some people believe that artificial intelligence will bring more benefits than problems to society. To what extent do you agree or disagree?**

- 用时：
- 词数：
- 自评：

### Outline

| 段落 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| Intro | Paraphrase topic + state position（agree with nuance） |
| Body 1 | Benefits — healthcare诊断, education个性化, automation释放人力 |
| Body 2 | Problems — job displacement, privacy, ethical concerns, bias |
| Body 3 | Balance — 关键在 governance/regulation，不是技术本身 |
| Conclusion | AI is net positive IF properly managed |

**高分词汇备选：**
- transformative potential
- double-edged sword
- mitigate risks
- ethical safeguards
- augment rather than replace
