**关键词方向：**

## 写作
- 类型：Task 1 — 图表描述（Line Graph）
- 题目：

> **The graph below shows the number of international students enrolled in online courses at a university between 2018 and 2024.**
>
> Summarize the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.

- 用时：
- 词数：
- 自评：

### Outline

| 段落 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| Intro | Paraphrase the graph title（不用抄原题） |
| Overview | 2-3 条核心趋势 — 不要数据，只要大方向 |
| Detail 1 | 哪几类增长最快？具体年份和数据 |
| Detail 2 | 对比：哪些持平/下降？交叉点在哪？ |
| (无 Conclusion) | Task 1 不需要总结段 |

**Task 1 高分词汇：**
- saw a steady increase / experienced a sharp rise
- remained relatively stable / fluctuated slightly
- peaked at / reached a low of
- accounted for / represented
- in contrast / compared to / whereas

**⚡ 强化提醒：** 限时 20 分钟。先 Overview 再 Detail。不要描述每一个数据点——挑最显著的 3-4 个特征。
