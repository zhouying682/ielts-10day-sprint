### 答题框架（Part 2 — Describe a piece of technology you find useful）

**关键词方向：**
- What: 技术/产品名称 + 一句话用途
- How: 怎么用 / 用多久了 / 频率
- Why useful: 解决了什么痛点（省时间/提高质量/连接资源）
- Negative: 诚实地提一个缺点 or 滥用风险（高分加分项）

**可用句型：**
- I've been using... for... and it's become absolutely essential for...
- Before I started using..., I used to... / I would struggle with...
- The biggest advantage is that it...
- That said, I'm also aware that... / One downside is...

**Part 3 追问预判：**
- How has technology changed the way people work in your country?
- Do you think older people are disadvantaged by new technology? → 正反两面说，准备数字鸿沟的例子
- What technology do you think will be most important in the next 10 years? → **AI + Web3，你的主场！但别用术语，用 layman's terms 解释**
- Should children be taught to use technology from a young age? → agree + limits

**💡 别踩的坑：** 提到 AI 时考官可能追问"AI 会取代你的工作吗"——准备一个体现 critical thinking 的回答（AI augments, doesn't replace... yet）。

## 写作
- 类型：**Task 1 — Bar Chart**
- 题目：

> **The chart below shows the percentage of people who used the internet for different online activities in three different age groups.**
>
> Summarize the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.

- 用时：
- 词数：
- 自评：

### Outline

| 段落 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| Intro | Paraphrase — 不用 percentage，用 proportion；不用 chart，用 bar chart / illustrates |
| Overview | 2 条最显著的 pattern：哪个 activities 全年龄段都高？哪个 age group 差异最大？ |
| Detail 1 | 年轻组表现（18-35）：哪些活动遥遥领先？数据点 |
| Detail 2 | 中老年对比（55+）：哪些活动明显低？和年轻组差距多少？有没有出乎意料的高点？ |
|（无 Conclusion）| Task 1 不需要总结段 |

**Bar Chart 专用词汇：**
- accounted for the highest proportion / was the most popular activity across all age groups
- showed a marked decline with age / the widest gap was observed in...
- at approximately X% / nearly X% / just over/under X%
- compared with / whereas / in stark contrast to

**⚡ 强化提醒：** 限时 20 分钟。Bar Chart 的核心是**比较**——对比不同 age group 在同一 activity 上的差异，不要逐个 activity 描述。Overview 给全局判断，Details 给数据支撑。150 字以上够用，别水到 250。
